About this Project

I built a dance pad controller for the game Friday Night Funkin' with Makey Makey Controller.

Work Process

Planning

I researched on the existing online games out there so I can decide on what kind of controller I want to make. I came across rhythm games and thought of making a dance pad like dance dance revolution.



I searched on how Makey Makey Controller works for a dance pad before proceeding on my own design. I made a proposal and in the document it is recorded down in detail about my project.



Download PDF

Designing

Wanted a budgeted and use of recycled materials to create my controller so I went to get cardboards, paint, tapes and aluminum foils to create my controller.

Results & Takeaways

Creating a controller with Makey Makey opened doors to a world of creativity and innovation. Through simple circuits and everyday objects, I transformed mundane items into interactive inputs, redefining the conventional notion of gaming controls. The hands-on experience of mapping inputs to actions sparked a deeper understanding of user interaction and interface design, emphasizing the importance of intuitive controls in enhancing gameplay experiences. Moreover, the project encouraged experimentation and exploration, fostering a spirit of ingenuity and resourcefulness in problem-solving. Reflecting on the process, it highlighted the potential of Makey Makey to democratize game development by empowering creators of all skill levels to craft unique and personalized gaming experiences.
