Work Process

Planning

My team discussed on making a meaningful horror game to spread awareness on the rising bullying cases. We decided the school settings take place in the 90s where the player is an adventurous fellow from 2020 to explore and find out about the horrifying past on the school.

Since the school setting is in the past, we researched on the old schools on the visuals and appearance so we can start building the modular assets needed for the game.

Results & Takeaways

Crafting a 3D horror game was a journey that delved deep into the intricacies of storytelling, atmosphere, and player immersion. From conceptualizing spine-chilling narratives to designing eerie environments and unsettling soundscapes, every element played a crucial role in instilling fear and suspense. The process demanded careful attention to pacing, tension-building techniques, and player psychology, underscoring the importance of narrative coherence and gameplay mechanics in delivering impactful experiences. Reflecting on the project, it illuminated the profound impact of atmosphere and narrative intricacies in shaping player experiences, while inspiring a newfound respect for the craft of horror game design.
