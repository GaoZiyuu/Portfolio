Design a level and make a game using Raycast

A school assignment project to create a game on the given scenario which is player lost control of spaceship & crashed landed on an unknown planet, where the player objective will have to find way to repair spaceship and escape from planet.

About this Project

This assignment main goal is to design & document a 3D virtual level based on the scenario given and creation of a 3D virtual level with careful consideration of User Experience.

Work Process

Planning

From the given scenario, I decided to set the planet setting to Mars and worked on my level design document, where I put down all my brainstorming and research. In my document, I had wrote down the level abstract and constraints of my game, my bubble diagram and rough maps to give a clearer visual of how the game will be like overall.
