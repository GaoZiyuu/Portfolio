Group's integrated project on a VR game experience

My group built an immersive experience to bring awareness on mental health well-being.

About this Project

Our group decided to help spread awareness on mental health well-being as one of the theme required for this project is social good \ inclusive design, thus we decided to raise awareness via a game experience to tell users more about mental health.

Work Process

Planning

We decided working on mental health and each member have one mental disorder to work on. For me, I chose Bipolar Disorder as the mental disorder.
