Making an AR experience

I made an AR experience on the storybook Alice In Wonderland.

About this Project

Created an Augmented Reality Mobile Application to provide interaction points for engaging consumers with storytelling book on Alice In Wonderland.

Work Process

Planning

I decided working on the storybook 'Alice In Wonderland' because I wanted and wish for storybooks to be interactive and fun to read while learning. Thus I decided on working on AR experience in storybooks so it will make a fun and meaningful reading experience for consumers.

Below shows visual research that I had done before working on the project.

Designing

I decided to go for low poly and simple art style because I only had 4 weeks to work on this project and low poly is simple and matches the fairytale/ wonderland like theme.

Results & Takeaways

Creating my first AR project was a journey of discovery and innovation. I navigated through a steep learning curve, grasping concepts like spatial mapping and interactivity. Witnessing virtual elements blend seamlessly into reality was mesmerizing, sparking my imagination. Crafting a smooth user experience demanded careful optimization and consideration for different devices and environments. Overall, it was a rewarding experience that opened my eyes to the vast potential of AR technology.
