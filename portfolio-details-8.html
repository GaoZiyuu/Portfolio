Results & Takeaways

Through this project, I learnt to apply the appropriate workflow for real-time 3D productions and the appropriate modelling techniques for 3D designs. I also learnt how to make and use a trimsheet and apply this texture technique for my models. This project also taught me to use and bake the appropriate lighting to set the appropriate atmosphere for real-time 3D productions.
